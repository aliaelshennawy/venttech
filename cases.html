Why Choose Vent Tec?

Because we clean the areas you can’t see, leaving you fire-safe. Everyone knows that restaurant operators are often too busy to inspect the plenum and all the ductwork after each cleaning. Many hood cleaners take advantage of this and let their people take shortcuts — shortcuts that could burn down your restaurant. We force our employees to prove to us with detailed pictures that they cleaned all accessible parts of your grease exhaust system, every time.
